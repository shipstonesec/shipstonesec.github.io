About

Shipstone is specialized firm geared towards finding solutions to unique security problems. Customers leverage our years of expertise to solve the unsolvable and develop strong security programs with low overhead. We understand the need for realistic information security solutions and serve as industry leaders advocating responsible security and privacy practices for all organizations, big and small.

Our team members have been working in the computer security industry since 1996 and have many combined decades of experience in the military, civilian intelligence, humanitarian aid, and the financial sector. We speak eleven languages, have presented over a hundred conference talks, and published fourteen books ranging from technical references to historical fiction. We hold seventeen Masters degrees, two Ph.D.s, ten professional licenses, five current security clearances, and one active probation period.
